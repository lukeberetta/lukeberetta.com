Introduction

I co‑led the design of v2 alongside the founding designer, building a flexible AI‑powered block canvas that redefined how stories get built and consumed. The new product unlocked non‑linear storytelling, deep analytics, and AI across the full experience. Journey was acquired, and the v2 architecture became the foundation the acquiring team inherited.

Understanding the space

I audited tools like Notion, Pitch, and Coda to understand how the best products handle flexible content creation. The clearest insight: most competitors had ignored the reading experience entirely. How a story gets consumed was almost always an afterthought.

Design direction

Three principles shaped v2. A block canvas where structure emerges from content rather than being imposed by a grid. An onboarding arc with multiple entry points — templates, AI generation, and blank canvas — designed to meet creators at their level. And an automagical theming system that pulled brand identity from a domain, so every Journey felt intentional without requiring design skills.

Validation

We tested multiple approaches to the canvas and onboarding flow. The block canvas with guided entry points consistently outperformed alternatives — faster first publishes, less blank‑state abandonment, higher creator confidence.

Solution

Replacing the fixed slide grid with a fluid block system let creators mix text, video, embeds, and interactive elements freely. The result adapted to any screen without manual adjustment.

AI across the full loop

On the creation side, describe what you want and Journey drafts the structure. Inline tools let you refine any block in context. On the reading side, an Ask Anything panel lets recipients query the content directly. Each surface was designed to feel native, not bolted on.

Analytics

A redesigned dashboard showed creators exactly how their stories were being consumed — which blocks got attention, where readers dropped off, who engaged and for how long. Data that had previously been invisible became the feedback loop for every next Journey.

Spritz Finance enabled people to pay everyday bills with cryptocurrency, transforming a complex technology into a simple and trustworthy financial experience.

Intro

Spritz Finance set out to bridge the gap between decentralized finance and everyday financial needs. While cryptocurrencies offered new financial freedom, using them for routine expenses like utility bills remained complex and inaccessible for most users. The goal was to design an experience that translated blockchain mechanics into something familiar, intuitive, and trustworthy.

Role: Principal Product Designer

Timeline: Nov 2024 - Nov 2025

Team: Product, Engineering, Compliance, Marketing

Context & Challenge

Despite the promise of cryptocurrency, paying real‑world bills remained complex and intimidating. Users faced unfamiliar terminology, concerns about volatility, and limited trust in crypto‑powered financial services. Additionally, regulatory and compliance requirements introduced constraints that needed to be seamlessly integrated into the experience.

The challenge was to design a secure, intuitive, and scalable payment journey that allowed users to confidently settle real‑world bills using cryptocurrency while abstracting the underlying technical complexity.

My Contribution

Defined the product vision and end‑to‑end user experience, translating complex crypto mechanics into a simple, trustworthy financial product.

Established the information architecture and scalable interaction patterns that made multi‑step transactions feel intuitive and predictable.

Introduced a cohesive design system that brought consistency across flows and enabled rapid iteration as the product scaled.

Collaborated closely with engineering and compliance to align user needs with regulatory requirements, ensuring the product was both delightful and legally sound.

Championed trust and transparency, embedding clear exchange rates, fee visibility, and transaction feedback throughout every critical moment.

Influenced product strategy and roadmap, advocating for user‑centered priorities and ensuring the foundation could support long-term growth.

Design Direction

Abstraction of Complexity. Blockchain interactions such as wallet connections, gas fees, and asset conversions were simplified into familiar financial concepts.

Trust Through Transparency. Clear exchange rates, fees, and transaction statuses were surfaced at key moments to reinforce confidence.

Scalable Foundations. A modular architecture enabled integrations with multiple billers and expansion into new regions.

Trust Indicators. Clear visibility of fees and exchange rates to support informed decision‑making.

Payment Flow Diagram. A simplified end‑to‑end journey reducing cognitive load.

Design System Components. A cohesive component library enabling consistency and rapid product development.

Solution

The final solution delivered a trust‑centric payment experience that made crypto transactions feel familiar and effortless.

Unified Dashboard. Centralized financial overview providing clarity and control.

Streamlined Payment Flow. Progressive disclosure guiding users through bill payment with minimal friction.

Real‑Time Transaction States. Transparent feedback throughout the transaction lifecycle.

Responsive Experience. A consistent and accessible interface across desktop and mobile devices.

Impact

Increased successful bill payments by removing the friction that had previously blocked users from completing transactions end‑to‑end.

Reduced onboarding time by abstracting wallet setup and asset conversion into a guided, step‑by‑step flow that felt familiar rather than technical.

Enhanced user trust through consistent transparency around fees, rates, and transaction status at every critical moment.

Enabled expansion into new markets with a modular architecture that supported new billers and regions without requiring a redesign.

Accelerated development through a scalable design system that gave engineering a shared language and reduced back‑and‑forth on implementation.

Conversion Metrics Visualization. Improved payment success rates following the redesigned flow.

User Feedback. Positive customer sentiment reflecting increased trust and usability.

Scalable System Adoption. Design system foundations enabling rapid expansion into new markets.

Outro

Spritz reinforced the importance of designing for trust when introducing emerging technologies. By abstracting complexity and grounding the experience in familiar mental models, we enabled cryptocurrency to function as a practical means of everyday payment.
